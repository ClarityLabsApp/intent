Questions
What happens when my free messages run out?
You'll be prompted to subscribe. You keep all your conversation history and commitments. Nothing is lost.
Is this therapy?
No. INTENT is a philosophical tool inspired by Adlerian psychology. It is not a substitute for professional mental health support. If you are in crisis, please contact a professional.
What is Adlerian psychology?
Alfred Adler believed that people are not determined by their past or their circumstances — they choose how to respond. INTENT is built on this premise: your problems serve a purpose, and until you see what that purpose is, nothing changes. Popularised in the book The Courage to Be Disliked.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes. At any time through your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. You retain access until the end of your billing period.
